Our Journey

Our Story

👥 Founded by Riad & Sayem

GOLDEN MOMENTS was founded by Riad & Sayem with a vision to transform ordinary celebrations into extraordinary memories. Based in Arambagh, Hooghly, we have grown to become a trusted name in event management across West Bengal and beyond.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every client deserves celebrations that feel effortlessly perfect, while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism. Whether it's a grand corporate event, an intimate birthday, or an elaborate wedding, we approach each with creativity, care and attention to detail.

Today, we proudly serve clients at home and internationally, bringing joy and beautiful memories wherever we go.
